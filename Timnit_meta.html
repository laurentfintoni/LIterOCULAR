Who watches the admen?

Three articles published between late 2020 and spring 2021 in US publications detailing the sudden departure of AI researcher Timnit Gebru from her position within Google's Ethical AI team following the publication of a research paper she co-authored. While Google has claimed the decision was justified by Gebru's behavior, others - including Gebru - claim the company was threatened by the research and ethics advocacy.

Primary topics: Gebru Research Paper, Identity, Gender, Racism, AI.
